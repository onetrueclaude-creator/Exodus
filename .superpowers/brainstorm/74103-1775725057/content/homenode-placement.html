Where does a new player's homenode go?

When a player joins and picks a faction, they need a homenode in their quadrant. How is it assigned?

Option A: Nearest Unclaimed to Origin
P1 gets ring-0 (closest to origin, most valuable). P2 gets next closest. Late joiners land further out. Early = better position.
Option B: Random in Quadrant
Players placed randomly within available cells. More fair, but less strategic. Players can't choose position.

How are homenodes assigned?

A

Nearest Unclaimed to Origin (Recommended)

First come, first served. Early players get prime real estate near the center. Late players land on the frontier. Creates urgency to join early.

B

Random Placement

Assigned randomly among unclaimed cells in the quadrant. More fair, but removes the strategic land-rush dynamic.

C

Player Picks Cell

Player clicks any unclaimed cell in their quadrant to place their homenode. Most agency, but adds a UI step to onboarding.
