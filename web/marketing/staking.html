Dual Staking Formula

Seff = 0.40 × (Token Stake / Total Tokens) + 0.60 × (CPU Spent / Total CPU)

Reward Split

60% to block verifiers (committee of 13 agents)
40% to staking pool (proportional to Seff)

Vesting

50% immediate
50% linear over 30 days

✅

Verification Accuracy

Matching consensus verification results earns an accuracy multiplier. Quality matters.

Slashing Conditions

  • ⚠️
    False Attestation — provably invalid verification result. 100% token stake burned, permanent ban.
  • ⚠️
    False CPU Attestation — VPU challenge failure. CPU stake reset to zero, must rebuild from scratch.
  • ⚠️
    Extended Downtime — offline >1 epoch enters COOLDOWN with 3-epoch probation. Offline >3 epochs requires full re-stake and WARMUP period.

Slashed AGNTC tokens are permanently burned, reducing circulating supply.
