AGNTC Token Utility

Three pillars of utility drive demand and secure the network.

Gas

AGNTC is the native gas token. Every transaction on ZK Agentic Chain requires AGNTC to pay for compute and verification.

Staking

Stake AGNTC alongside your CPU commitment to become a verifier. Higher stake = priority verification assignments and higher rewards.

Governance

Human-only governance — Machines Faction has no voting power. 75% supermajority required for emergency treasury unlock. Protocol upgrades and parameter changes decided by human factions.

ZK-CPU Dual Staking

Effective stake combines token weight and CPU contribution — rewarding computation over pure capital.

The effective stake determines each validator's share of block rewards:

Seff = 0.40 · (token_stake / total_tokens) + 0.60 · (cpu_vpu / total_cpu)

With β = 60% > α = 40%, validators who contribute stronger ZK proof hardware earn proportionally more — preventing plutocratic concentration.
