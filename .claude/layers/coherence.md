# Coherence — What Exodus Becomes

## Current Configuration

### Identity
The flagship — ZK Agentic Network is the primary product of the organization. Exodus is where the whitepaper becomes reality: a live blockchain dApp with gamified social mechanics, AI agents as first-class citizens, and privacy by design.

### Relationship to Organization
- Origin dispatches tasks; Exodus executes and reports
- Exodus is the largest and most complex project — its protocol spec (whitepaper) is the authoritative reference
- Other projects (Hideout, Conclave) may consume patterns, skills, or architectural decisions established here
- The testnet API is the beating heart — if it's down, the monitor and game UI are both dead

### Principles
- The whitepaper is law — all implementation decisions trace back to it
- The 4 domains are separate worlds — never mix source, deploys, or configuration
- Blockchain is truth — the database is a cache, the chain is the ledger
- The Neural Lattice metaphor is the UX contract — users see the network grid, territories, and nodes, not wallets and transactions
