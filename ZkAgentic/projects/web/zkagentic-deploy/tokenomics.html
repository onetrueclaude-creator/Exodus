ZK-CPU Dual Staking

Effective stake combines token weight and CPU contribution — rewarding computation over pure capital.

The effective stake determines each validator's share of block rewards:

Seff = 0.35 · (token_stake / total_tokens) + 0.65 · (cpu_vpu / total_cpu)

With β = 65% > α = 35%, validators who contribute stronger ZK proof hardware earn proportionally more — preventing plutocratic concentration.
